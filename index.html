📖 How to Play

Master the art of battle before you face the Oracle

⚔ Combat Actions
⚔️
Attack 15–25 DMG
Strike the enemy. If they defend, you get countered. If they heal, you deal full damage. Chain attacks for Combos!
🛡️
Defend Counter 8–16
Block incoming attacks and deal a counter strike. If the enemy attacks, you get a Perfect Block!
💚
Heal +25–35 HP
Restore your health. If the enemy attacks while you heal, you take damage too. Limited uses!
🧠 The Oracle AI
📊
The Oracle uses a MLP weight matrix to track and predict your moves. The more you repeat patterns, the more accurately it counters you!
👁
Watch the Prediction Bars — they show the AI's real-time confidence of what you'll do next. Fool it to win!
🎯
Be unpredictable. Mix up your moves. The AI adapts every single round based on your history.
🎒 Inventory Items
🧪
Elixir +40–60 HP — Instant heal, stronger than the Heal action.
💣
Bomb 30–50 DMG — Explosive instant damage outside of normal turns.
🔮
Rune Regen ×3 — Regenerate HP for 3 turns automatically.
🪬
Ward Shield ×3 — Reduce incoming damage by 70% for 3 turns.
🏆 Scoring & Tips
⚡
Combos — Chain Attacks to multiply damage. Each combo adds +5 bonus damage.
🔥
Streak — Win rounds in a row for a score multiplier bonus.
📈
Levels — Defeat each enemy to advance. Enemies get stronger every level!
💡
Pro Tip: Watch the AI's prediction bars. When it's 60%+ on Attack — Defend! When it expects Defend — Attack!
